About Me

I'm more than a UI Designer. I have the knowledge and passion that can contribute to any stage of a project. I care about the process and the final product. Most of all, I care about the users.

I design with the final product and the users in mind. You'll see the same steps of the process being followed in my portfolio as you'll see in others. The difference is my thoughtfulness and attention to detail. I design with purpose. It's important to me to understand the 5 w's of each project. I design with Nielsen's 10 Usability Heuristics in mind to limit as many loose ends as possible. I give the extra thought towards a concept to assure the user is getting the product that represents their needs best. That's why I don't rush through the steps and embrace the benefits of feedback and revisions that other designers are fearful of.

My Experience

For 10+ years I was a production artist in the print industry. My role was to transform designs into finished products. Through this role I learned how design and production go hand in hand. Designs must be compatible with the production or development process. This experience transfers over to UI/UX very well. I know that the designs I create must work for developers and translate into a user friendly finished product.

Through the years I've completed a couple of degree and certificate programs related to digital media design. In 2022, I enrolled in the UI Design Program at CareerFoundry where I narrowed my focus. This is where I worked on industry centered projects. I learned about the UI design process. During the program I refined my existing visual design skills and added new tools and processes to my skill set.

Most importantly, my time at CareerFoundry allowed me to build a professional portfolio and my confidence. I grew tremendously and so did my passion for UI/UX.
